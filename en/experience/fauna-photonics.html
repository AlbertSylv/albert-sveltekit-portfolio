FaunaPhotonics · Bachelor

Digital concept development · insect data portal · 2023

Bachelor thesis with FaunaPhotonics. User interviews and a Figma prototype for the insect data portal.

Why it mattered

FaunaPhotonics was rebuilding their insect data portal for the 2024 season. They needed product direction, not just a visual refresh.

What I did

Interviewed customers already using the portal (Zoo København, Hedeselskabet, Better Energy, Clever), mapped findings against the business case around emerging biodiversity reporting, and prototyped the new portal end-to-end in Figma.

The insight that drove the design

Raw insect counts don't tell a story on their own. Users need comparison: across sensors, sites and seasons. The prototype puts the session list in every view and makes session-to-session comparison a first-class action.
